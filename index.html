Biography.

Anna Menzies emerged from the wilds of Owen Sound, ON with a proficiency for bleakly deadpan pronouncements regarding her education and prospects. Her talent for detached self-mockery is matched with a gift for presenting awkward situations in a bad light before finding another angle that's even worse. It’s from this place of comedy, she was able to become the runner up in the 2017 Comedy Brawl.
